Use Photoshop to create a mock-up of a digital portfolio.

  • Download the modular grid (4x4) and open it in Photoshop.
  • Open an existing image and make a square selection from it, copy it, then paste this selection into your grid. Repeat this with a number of other images.
  • Use the grid to help you size the images and create the layout.
  • Try making a 200px x 200px selection and pasting a new image into it. Move the image around inside the selection and resize it.
  • Move the images around the grid and group them in different ways.
  • Put all of the image layers into a folder, duplicate it and rearrange the images in a different layout.
