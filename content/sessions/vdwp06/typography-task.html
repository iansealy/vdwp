Styling type in your mockup.

  • Choose 2 fonts and create a 'text tree' (h1, h2, h3, h4, subheading and body).
  • Apply styles from your 'text tree' to selections of text on your mockup.
  • Use a colour from your swatch and apply it to a selection of text in your mockup.
