Applying colour to your mockup.

  • Try changing the colour of existing shapes in your Photoshop mockup file.
  • Draw new shapes on to the base image using the rectangle tool.
  • Try changing the colour of the shapes. You can use a swatch downloaded from Adobe Color CC or Paletton.
